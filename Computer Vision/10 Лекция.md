Матрица Гессе
$$
\begin{gather}
\delta\left(  \frac{\delta f}{\delta x} \right) = \frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta x^{2}} =\\
=\frac{\delta\left( \frac{\delta f}{\delta x} \right)}{\delta y}=\frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta x\delta y}
\end{gather}
